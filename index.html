I’m a recently graduated Solar physicist, currently as a post-doc at the Max Planck Institute for Solar System Research in Göttingen, Germany. I am interested in studying the interiors of the Sun and Stars through observations and simulations. My doctoral research has been in inferring the solar rotation rate near the poles from observations of the newly-discovered solar inertial mode. You can find out more about the projects I've worked on in the 'Projects' tab, and my CV under the 'Vitae' tab.
